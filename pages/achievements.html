Achievements

Team of 2024 participated at iGEM 2024 and won Best Human Practices in the All India iGEM Meet.

They won a Silver Medal at the GoGec Conference 2025.

They cleared the internal round of the Smart India Hackathon 2025 under the Hardware category.

Team of 2023 won a Gold medal at iGEM and a 2500$ Impact grant.

They also won a Gold Medal, Best Human Centered Design, Best Project Value & Best Experimental Project at GoGec Conference 2024.

Team of 2022 won a Gold medal at the iGEM Jamboree and an impact grant of $2500.

They were nominated for Best Education, Best Presentation, Best Software Tool and Best Wiki in Overgrad.

They were also nominated for Best Food & Nutrition Award in both Undergrad and Overgrad.

They were placed 6th out of 56 Teams at GoK Biohackathon 2022.

Our inaugural team in 2020 won a Gold Medal at iGEM.
